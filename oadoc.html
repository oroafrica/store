Proposed Navison Web Services.
Overview
Proposed objective is to implement a digital workflow that takes into account the various challenges in the order placement process. This include but not limited to checking stock levels prior to moving to production, apply client specific changes and extend overall validation. Standardize the format of imported data from external clients and provide a point of reference for sales, distribution and finance. With the overal intend to embed the various individual task into a single delegable process.
The process will be devided into three phases
Implementation/Testing of rest services Development of client Payment/Order reconciliation
Timeline
AllOnline: 15 Hours Orodesign: 3 weeks
Prism
Prism will act as middleware to facilitate required processes and hosted on a local server. Encoding UTF-8. Preferred http response format json.
Order Capturing
Prism client will be entry point for webservice or excel spreadsheet uploads. Orders will be pre-processed and assessed prior to uploading to Navision this include payment filtering. This include but no limited to sold from stock or production orders.
Finance
Payment vendor API's will be used to aid in transaction tracking where payment disparaties arise. Tracking method limited to services exposed exposed by payment vendor.
Sales
Quick access to the following: Memi product information. Query orders by status/sales order. Log request and receive responses eg. previews/translations

Navision Rest Services

  • Query client information including account status for account payments by account/name
